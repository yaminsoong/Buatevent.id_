Check out our FAQ section for quick answers to common inquiries. We’re committed to making your event planning experience seamless and stress-free.
How does BuatEvent.id work?
Simply enter your event needs, including the type of venue, location, transportation, budget, and number of attendees. BuatEvent.id will generate customized recommendations, and you can select and adjust items before checking out and making a payment.
Can I adjust my budget after receiving recommendations?
Yes, you can adjust your budget after receiving recommendations to find the best options that fit your needs.
What types of events can I plan on BuatEvent.id?
BuatEvent.id supports planning for a wide range of events, including corporate gatherings, weddings, and parties.
Is my payment secure on BuatEvent.id?
Yes, all payments made through BuatEvent.id are secured using the latest encryption technologies.
Will I receive confirmation after payment?
Yes, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details of your order after payment is completed.
Is there customer support available if I need help?
Absolutely! Our customer support team is available to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.
